Hobbies



▼ Writing Code


In the last few years, I have learnt to code in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, SQL and Java. This has allowed me to develop many pieces including websites, Android apps and Java applications.

▼ Creating 3D Art


I have been developing 3D art since January of 2016 using Blender. I have been uploading my latest places to Instagram since the Summer of 2019. These pieces are available at: @henry_james_art
I hope some day to be able to sell my art or use it in commercial products.

▼ Kayaking


I have been kayaking passionately every week since early 2019. I am currently the Treasurer of the University of Kent Kayaking Club. I use this position to try and promote and teach kayaking to new people. Recently, I was awarded with the Start and Discover Paddle Award for paddling in Alps white-water.

▼ Fitness


Physical and mental fitness has been a huge passion of mine for many years. I am very interested in how good health can improve happiness and focus. I regularly attend the gym and kayaking. I have also experimented with other sports like rugby, karate and dodgeball in my fitness journey.

▼ Learning


I have been fascinated in learning and developing my skills since my childhood. I love to learn in a variety of areas. At the moments, I am developing my knowledge in:
 - Technology:

  • Through my course in Computer Science at the University of Kent and through my personal projects developing software.
 - Sport
  • I love to learn and begin to master new sports. I am currently learning all there is to know about kayaking. In the last six months I have learnt the basics and am beginning to learn more advanced techniques like rolling the kayak.
 - Languages
  • For the past six months I have been learning French. This has broadened my understanding of the world and has allowed me to think in a broader manner than before. I am hoping to become fluent before learning more languages.
